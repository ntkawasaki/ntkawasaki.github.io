Why You Should Consider Me

Strengths, Interests

I believe that my strengths and personal interests in Looker align perfectly with the qualities needed for the Applcations Team Data Analyst position. While I have only had 8 months in total experience at Looker, I have demonstrated a level of proficiency in all technical areas involved in developing, maintaining, and applying LookML projects to business needs.

My greatest strength, and what I would consider my "superpower" if I had one, is my ability and willingness to learn. Over the past three years, I began three activities mostly as hobbies, yet that ended up teaching me something much more valuable: how to learn. They are: Wingsuit Skydiving, Crossfit, and programming. To quickly summarize the effect in which these three hobbies helped me learn how to learn, they all take a looong amount of time, a genuine belief in attainment, and persistence. The acceptance that progress in any subject takes time, yet know that progress will happen as long as you put in persistent effort, is a mindset that I can and would be able to apply to the projects in the Applications Team role. In all honesty I do not have that much experience with the Applications that you have already created, nor do I know all of our blocks inside and out, but I am confident that these are projects I can quickly become familiar with and even build upon.

My interests in technology and Looker can be boiled down to the desire to build solutions that empower people. With respect to Looker only, LookML modeling and development is by far my favorite part of Looker for a couple of reasons. The first is simply that it is fun and easy to write. The second, and main reason I enjoy writing LookML is because of what it enables other people to do. I have been using Looker for 8 months now, and so I have become used to what it does. Yet when I see how people react to their first sales demo or explain it to a friend I remember how incredible of a solution it is to be able to give analytical power to people who have zero SQL or data backgrounds. As I see it, the Applications Team is an extension of this empowerment. It gives people analytical power from the beginning of implementation. Because of how my personal interest in Looker aligns with the goal of Applications, I would fully engage myself in projects and passionately think of ways to further empower our customers in data analytics.
